Strawberry custard

Here’s a simple recipe to make strawberry custard: Ingredients: 2 cups milk 2 tablespoons custard powder (vanilla or plain) 3–4 tablespoons sugar (to taste) 1 cup fresh strawberries, chopped Instructions: In a small bowl, mix custard powder with 1/4 cup cold milk to make a smooth paste. Heat the remaining milk in a saucepan over medium heat. Add sugar and stir until dissolved. When the milk begins to boil, lower the heat and slowly add the custard paste, stirring continuously. Cook for 2–3 minutes, stirring, until the mixture thickens. Remove from heat and let it cool to room temperature. Add chopped strawberries and mix well. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour and serve chilled. Enjoy your creamy, fruity strawberry custard!

Egg Curry

Here’s a simple recipe to make egg curry: Ingredients: 4–6 hard-boiled eggs, peeled 2 tablespoons oil 1 onion, finely chopped 2 tomatoes, chopped (or 1 cup tomato puree) 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder 1 teaspoon red chili powder 1 teaspoon coriander powder 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds 1/2 teaspoon garam masala Salt to taste Fresh coriander leaves for garnish Instructions: Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Add chopped onions. Sauté until golden brown. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and fry for a minute. Add tomatoes, turmeric, chili, and coriander powder. Cook until tomatoes soften and oil separates. Add salt and a little water for gravy. Simmer for 2–3 minutes. Gently add boiled eggs (whole or halved) and simmer for 5–7 minutes, letting them soak up the flavors. Sprinkle garam masala and mix gently. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or roti. Enjoy your home-style egg curry!

Smoky Chicken

Here’s a simple recipe to make smoky chicken: Ingredients: 500g chicken pieces (with skin for best results) 2 tablespoons olive oil 1 teaspoon smoked paprika 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder 1/2 teaspoon onion powder 1/2 teaspoon chili powder (optional, for heat) Salt and pepper to taste Juice of half a lemon Fresh herbs for garnish (optional) Instructions: In a bowl, mix olive oil, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, chili powder, salt, pepper, and lemon juice. Coat the chicken pieces thoroughly with the spice mixture. Marinate for at least 30 minutes (or up to 4 hours in the fridge). Preheat your grill or oven to 200°C (400°F). Grill or bake the chicken for 25–30 minutes, turning occasionally, until cooked through and slightly charred for that smoky flavor. Let rest a few minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh herbs if desired. Enjoy flavorful, smoky chicken with minimal effort)

Mushroom green stir fry

Here’s a simple and healthy mushroom and greens stir-fry recipe: Ingredients: 200g mushrooms, sliced 2 cups fresh green leaves (like spinach, kale, or Swiss chard) 1 tablespoon olive oil 2 garlic cloves, minced Salt and pepper to taste A squeeze of lemon juice (optional) Instructions: Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté for 30 seconds until fragrant. Add sliced mushrooms and cook for 4–5 minutes until they soften and release their moisture. Add the green leaves and sauté for another 2–3 minutes until wilted. Season with salt, pepper, and a squeeze of lemon juice if desired. Toss and serve warm. This dish is quick, nutritious, and full of earthy flavors—perfect as a side or a light main!)
